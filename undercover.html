Undercover

A fast social deduction game for parties. Most players see the secret word. One (or more) players do not. Talk smart, stay calm, and spot the imposters before they blend in.

What is the game?

Undercover is designed for in-person groups. Each round takes only a few minutes, which makes it easy to play again and again.

How does it work?

Step 1

Secret Roles

Everyone receives a role card. Most players get a word, imposters do not.

Step 2

Discussion

Players give clues and listen closely while the round timer runs.

Step 3

Vote

Each player votes. The group tries to eliminate the real imposter.

Step 4

Reveal

Roles and the word are shown. Then start the next fast round.
